# Runway Gen‑4.5: The World's Most Powerful Video Generation Model Elevates Cinematic Production with AI

On 1 December 2025, Runway announced the launch of the Gen‑4.5 model, the new model that became the world’s number one in AI‑generated video. The model holds 1247 Elo points on the Artificial Analysis Text‑to‑Video scale, outperforming all competitors including models from Google and OpenAI. Gen‑4.5 focuses on motion quality, adherence to text descriptions, and unprecedented visual fidelity, while maintaining the speed and efficiency of its predecessor Gen‑4.

The model is an advanced video generation model within the Runway platform, and it supports full control modes such as Text‑to‑Video, Image‑to‑Video, Keyframes, and Video‑to‑Video. It produces realistic cinematic videos at high resolution, with accurate physics and natural motion, making it suitable for beginners who want to produce professional content quickly, for professionals in advertising and filmmaking, and for project owners who seek to reduce media production costs without sacrificing quality.

## Evolution of Runway Models and Gen‑4.5’s Position

Runway began its journey with Gen‑1 in 2023 as the first publicly available general video model, then evolved to Gen‑3 and Gen‑4 which focused on consistency and control. Gen‑4.5 represents the next step, based on improvements in training‑data efficiency and post‑training techniques.

The fundamental difference lies in physical accuracy: objects move with real weight and momentum, fluids flow with natural dynamics, and lighting and shadows match reality. The model also fully supports Image‑to‑Video as of January 2026, allowing any image to be turned into an animated video with precise camera and motion control.

## Key Technical Features

Runway Gen‑4.5 has specifications that make it practically superior:

- **Motion & Physics Quality**: Unprecedented physical accuracy in weight, momentum, and force, with realistic simulation of motion and fluids.
- **Prompt Adherence**: Understands complex text descriptions with high precision without losing details.
- **Visual Fidelity**: Realistic, high‑resolution cinematic production with consistency in characters, locations, and style.
- **Full Control Modes**: Text‑to‑Video, Image‑to‑Video, Keyframes, Video‑to‑Video, and Motion Brush.
- **Speed & Efficiency**: Maintains Gen‑4’s performance without increased wait time or cost.
- **Duration & Resolution**: Supports videos up to 10 seconds or more depending on settings, at high resolution.

The model supports all current Runway features and is available directly through the application and API.
